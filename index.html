How it Works

-1-

Employers Sign up

Sign up, select an annual benefit package, and choose between our monthly or annual payment options. Don’t worry about paying for services your employees don’t use! The value of any unused services at the end of the benefit year will be deducted from the cost of the benefit package you select for the following year.

-2-

Employees Sign up

Direct your employees visit to our website where they’ll create a profile and learn more about their new benefits!

-3-

Employees Access Care

Employees in need of care will visit our website, choose a provider they want to see, and walk-in or schedule an appointment. When your employee checks in for the visit, she (he) will redeem a virtual token, which will fully cover the cost of the services included in her (his) benefit package.

What We Do

We work directly with healthcare providers to offer packages of healthcare services. We do not offer insurance, rather, we make it easy and affordable for you cover the cost of basic healthcare services for your employees.

What We Offer

For as little as $25 a month per employee, you can provide your employees access to two clinical visits (including instant lab tests) and special pricing on certain services not included in your benefit package. We offer several benefit options and structure them so that you don’t pay for unused services.

Who Do We Serve

Our benefit packages are a great fit for employers with part-time employees, employers with high-deductible-health-plans (HDHP), and employers that don’t currently offer health benefits.
